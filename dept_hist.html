Professor Wing-hung Wong served as the chairman of the Department. 1998 Professor Yeh Lam succeeded Professor Wing-hung Wong and took up the chairmanship of the Department. 1999 Risk Management Science (RMSC) stream was launched. Professor Kim Hung Li assumed the chairmanship for one year. 2000 Professor Jian Qing Fan served as the seventh chairman of the Department. Risk Management Science (RMSC) was expanded to be an individual programme of study. The first batch of students majoring in RMSC was enrolled. 2004 Professor Ngai Hang Chan was inaugurated as the Department chairman. 2009 The Department first offered a double-major programme in Quantitive Finance and Risk Management Science (QRFM). 2012 Corresponding to the new 3-3-4 curriculum, the undergraduate programme in Statistics joined Science Faculty broad-based admission scheme. 2013 Professor Qi Man Shao was inaugurated as the Department chairman. 2018 Professor Xinyuan Song was inaugurated as the Department chair.
